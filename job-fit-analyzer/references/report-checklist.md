# Report Checklist

Use this checklist before presenting the final report. If any item fails, fix the report and re-check the failed item.

## Required Checks

- JD Quality Assessment appears before the match score.
- All seven JD quality checks have specific findings, not generic filler.
- Problem Diagnosis maps each major JD duty to an inferred business problem and candidate evidence.
- Overall score equals the weighted sum of the five dimension scores after penalties.
- The report uses exactly five scoring dimensions.
- Every dimension includes a detailed scoring breakdown with formula, sub-scores, evidence, and rationale.
- Experience scoring shows years, seniority, relevance, and all penalty deductions.
- Keyword coverage includes priority-weighted JD keywords and distinguishes demonstrated vs. listed-only evidence.
- Strengths and weaknesses are specific to this JD and resume.
- Recommendations include exactly three categories: Resume Tailoring, Skill Gap Mitigation, and Application Strategy.
- Recommendations contain no interview preparation content.
- All `{{...}}` placeholders from `assets/report-template.html` are replaced.
- Language matches the dominant language of the JD/resume.

## Fix Loop

1. Note each failed item and the affected report section.
2. Fix the report content or calculation.
3. Recalculate scores if scoring evidence changed.
4. Re-run the checklist until every item passes.
